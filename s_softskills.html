What are soft skills?

Soft skills are personal attributes, related to language, cognitive and emotional skills.

The importance of soft skills lies in the fact that they are not restricted to a specific field.

Their ductility helps individuals to adapt and behave positively so that they can deal effectively with the challenges of their professional and everyday life.

Soft skills make people flexible in a world which keeps changing.

Soft Skills:

  • Teamwork and Collaboration

    I have worked well with many people of different work and cultural backgrounds. I have collaborated on many successfull projects.

  • Leadership and Project management

    I have managed various teams to successfully complete projects and presentations. I am able to set up reasonable timelines and make sure our team's vision aligns with the scope of our project. I am proficient in Trello to manage my teams.

  • Empathy and emotional intelligence

    I am able to connect with my team members, and provide encouragement and assurance. Because of this human connection, the workplace is generally happier and more efficient.

  • Critical thinking

    I approach challenges with a unique perspective that ensures any problem is solved effectively.
